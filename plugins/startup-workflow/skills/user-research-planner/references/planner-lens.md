# Planner Lens

This stage exists to bridge opportunity framing and evidence review.

Its job is to make sure the product manager collects the right evidence in the first place.

Prioritize these outputs:

1. validation hypotheses
2. strict participant screener
3. behavioral interview guide
4. evidence-friendly note template
5. evidence summary template

Prefer method mixes like:

- `8-12` deep interviews for scenario understanding
- `20-50` lightweight survey responses for frequency and replacement patterns
- focused desk research when public traces can reduce ambiguity

Do not overbuild the research plan. The goal is to resolve the main uncertainty, not to create a large research program.
